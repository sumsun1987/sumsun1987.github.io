【纯音乐】the truth that you leave

365天 纯音乐 2019/03/31

I have not seen you for 10 years.I do not have a picture of you.But you live in my memory. It’s not good.But that was the happiest time of my life.I think I can’t see you again.you’re somewhere in the world. It’s enough.I have my own life. I am looking for something that makes my life amusing.

I have accepted the truth that you leave.
