【语录】主角永远不会死的

365天 2019/04/10

life is like a book.

you cannot skip chapters.

you have to read every line, meet every charapter.

you won’t enjoy all of it.

some chapters will make u cry for weeks.

you will read things u don’t want to read.

you will have moments you don’t want to pages to end.

you gotta believe God puts a character in your life for a reason.

And removes him from your life for a better reason.

Characters come and leave,They die.

But the leading roles never die.

And We are all leading roles in our lives.
